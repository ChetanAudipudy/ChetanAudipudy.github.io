About Me

I am an aspiring full stack developer and a designer. Tinkering with computers and software has always fascinated me from a very young age. It started with messing around with the code of games and trying to find hacks or workarounds. It eventually evolved into finding flaws or bugs with different softwares. I got into building computers after I built my first computer in 2008. My Engineering degree in Electronics also acted as a catalyst to this. Soon after, I found myself trying to mess with my first android phone by changing roms and kernels. I've always had the curiosity to find how things work and always sought more efficient solutions. I took a course in Java and android app development. Now I am trying to expand into web development aswell. I play a lot of sports like Cricket, Basketball and Chess. As a hobby I solve the Rubick's Cube. My best solve time on the Rubick's Cube 3x3 is 15.2 seconds. I'm an avid gamer too. Participated in multiple online tournaments in games such as Counter-Strike, DOTA 2 and Rocket League.
